Q 1. What is the strength of the SINGHA BAHENI Harnesses?

Ans. The SINGHA BAHENI CE range of harnesses are tested as per procedures laid down in the relevent EN norms to sustain a static load of 15 KN for three minutes without showing signs of damage of the webbing or metal parts.

Q 2. What is the shelf life of SINGHA BAHENI Harnesses?

Ans. When stored in ideal conditions(as specified in the instructional manual) the shelf life of SINGHA BAHENI harnesses is 5 years.

Q 3. What is the work life of the SINGHA BAHENI Harnesses?

Ans. The SINGHA BAHENI harness is a personal protective equipment. It should be used till as long as there are no visible signs of wear or damage to any part of the harness.It has been observed that when the SINGHA BAHENI harness is used in the correct manner and stored in clean environment (as per storage and maintenance guidelines given in the instruction manual) the harness can last for up to 10 years.

Q 4. Can I wash my SINGHA BAHENI Harness?

Ans. It is important to keep the harness clean and away from grime and dust. You may wash the harness using a mild soap(neutral Ph) and a soft sponge. Also it is important to dry the harness naturally away from direct sunlight.

Q 5. What is the maximum weight of a worker suitable for wearing SINGHA BAHENI Harness?

Ans. SINGHA BAHENI harnesses are tested on 100 kg dummy mass when using maximum 2m lanyard.

Q 6. Which SINGHA BAHENI Harnesses are ideal for use with SINGHA BAHENI easy seat IMP 004?

Ans. Easy Seat IMP 004 can be used with SINGHA BAHENI harnesses – Magna 3 and Rhino harnesses PN 56 and PN 57.

Q 7. How do I know that the intended life of SINGHA BAHENI Lanyard is over?

Ans. SINGHA BAHENI rope lanyards are provided with colored tracer strands. These strands fade when exposed to ultra-violet degradation hence indicating possible damage and weakening of the rope lanyard. This way the user can know that the lanyard is now not fit for further use.

FALL ARRESTERS

Q 1. Can we use SINGHA BAHENI Rope Grab Fall Arrester PN 2000 with any Anchorage Line other than SINGHA BAHENI Polyamide Anchorage Line?

Ans. SINGHA BAHENI rope- grab fall arrester PN 2000 is a guided-type fall arrester on a flexible anchorage line and is certified as per EN 353-2. SINGHA BAHENI recommends that the rope-grab be used only along with SINGHA BAHENI polyamide anchorage line of 14mm or 16mm dia because it has been tested and certified in this combination only. If used with any other anchorage line the product cannot be considered as a certified equipment. SINGHA BAHENI shall not be liable for any consequences incurred thereafter.

Q 2. What is the frequency of servicing for the SINGHA BAHENI Retractable Blocks?

Ans. SINGHA BAHENI recommends that the SINGHA BAHENI retractable fall arrester blocks be sent to SINGHA BAHENI authorized service centres for inspection and servicing each time the equipment is arrested to a fall. Additionally SINGHA BAHENI also recommends annual servicing of blocks which are in use.

Q 3. What is the advantage of the Swivel Eye on the SINGHA BAHENI Blocks?

Ans. The swivel anchorage eye provided on the SINGHA BAHENI retractable blocks allows the block to move along with the user without causing undue twist of the rope. Hence in the event of a fall the victim does not swivel dangerously while suspended at a height.

ANCHORAGES

Q 1. What can i use for safe Anchorage where the Termination Hook of my Lanyard is unable to provide the required opening?

Ans. SINGHA BAHENI offers an entire range of anchorage slings of various lengths. These slings can easily be wound around strong support structures(taking care that there are no sharp edges) hence providing ready anchorage points.

Q 2. How can I get safe Anchorage on structures like I-Beams?

Ans. SINGHA BAHENI provides a ready solution for anchorage on beams through its product beam anchor reference SA 08. The adjustable arm of the anchor post allows usage on beams of various widths.

Q 3. How can we get safe Anchorage over the lenght of a roof?

Ans. SINGHA BAHENI provides a portable easy to use temporary horizontal anchorage line PN 3000 which is available in a total length of 20 m. This line can be adjusted for lengths ranging from 5m to 20m. You can use anchorage slings on the posts to connect this horizontal line at the two ends.
